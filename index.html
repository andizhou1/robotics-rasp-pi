Storyboard
Compared to other kinds of museums, art museums tend resist new technology. Ordinary people in an art museum usually feel emotionally separated - because art knowledge has always been hard to access and understand. The aim of Future Museums is to create immersive experience in art museums and make art knowledge more accessible and digestible.

On-screen Interaction

This application is designed for the electronic vibration touchscreens at the museums. Visitors can touch it to feel the texture of a painting. It is also a museum artwork database that enables the audience to look for artworks directly, and have brief introductions to them. It not only shows artworks that are exhibited, but also shows the ones in the museum storage.

Concept Proposal

Art student Jessica was facing a creativity block, and so she went to the museum for inspiration.

Future Museum Platform

The system provides an immersive, fun experience to people, both inside and outside. The core technologies enable people to access art knowledge easily, and pass on knowledge in a more sharable way. Supporting the efforts of contemporary art museums - restoration, exhibition, and preservation - are the main functions of Future Museums. But, in Future Museums, part of the museum experience could be taken home.
