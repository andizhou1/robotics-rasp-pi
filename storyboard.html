Storyboard
Jessica is an art student. She's frustrated because she is facing a creativity block.
She wants to find some inspiration through her desk pad.
A news feed pops out: Best art museum in town! She decides to go to the museum, so she saves this event to her calendar.
She puts on her AR lenses.
She is ready to go
On her way to the museum, Jessica is using her lenses to see which exhibitions are most interesting.
Jessica arrives and walks into the museum, wandering.
When she is looking at an impressionism painting, the information pops out from the lenses and she scrolls to see more.
Jessica takes photo of the painting with her lenses and adds notes and sketches on the picture.
At the end of the show, she sees a touchscreen.
She interacts with the touch screen. She picks the painting she likes the most and feel the texture through the screen.
She takes notes and quick sketch.
Jessica wanders in a hallway and AR sculptures are walking around.
She encountered a AR showroom. The whole room shows a 3D Jackson Pollock painting.
She walks in the room. People inside become Pollock painting style. There's also a VR Pollock there and she talks to him.
Pollock says she may want to have a look at some modern sculptures. She passes the library where there are people reading.
Jessica enters the modern sculpture exhibition.
She walks closer to one sculpture...
Her lenses tell her that there's an interactive game that she can play with, using the same material of the sculpture and build her own one
While she is playing, she also looks at her previous notes from time to time. When she finally finished, an appointment alarm pops out.
She goes back home because she has a meeting with her artwork partner. She shows her friend the sculpture she just made and her friend wants to go to that museum the other day!
With Jessica's sketches, notes and sculpture experiment, they are happily discussing their art project because there is so much inspiration now.
